# Flet Arduino Sweeper Car -- Ca-R-oomba 🚓

**Click on this [link](https://github.com/ArifNaufalMNazri/Flet-Arduino-Sweeper-Car--Ca-R-oomba) to view the repository on <b>Github</b> for a more in depth breakdown.** 👁️

## Tell you why
I wanted to learn a bit of app development while simultaneously building something that could be remote-controlled. A car seemed like the perfect fit. However, after seeing the massive volume of bluetooth-controlled arduino cars, I wanted to make mine different. What I did was:
- Develop my own controller app using Python and Flet
- Control the car using the serial port
- Add sweeping functionality to it

## Hardware and Software 
- **Hardware**: Car chassis and motors from online shop, Arduino, Servo motors, HC-SR04, Motor Driver, Li-ion and AAA Batteries
- **Software**: Python, Flet, Arduino IDE
- **Skills**: Hardware debugging and wiring, Troubleshooting multiple systems, Communication between software and hardware
